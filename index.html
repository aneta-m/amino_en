ULTRA-FAST
NUTRITION EFFECT

AMINO ULTRA® are innovative, micronutrient fertilizer intended for foliar feeding. AMINO ULTRA® are based on the patented GCAA technology, in which microelements are bonded with the amino acid - glycine. Glycine is the smallest and the most mobile amino acid in plants. Therefore, the micronutrients bonded with glycine ensure faster and more effective plant nutrition than mineral salts, oxides or fertilizers based on other complexing agents. Thanks to the innovative technology, all nutrients are contained in one ultra-granule.

Fertilizers for NEW CHALLENGES in agriculture

Nowadays, one of the greatest challenges faced by farmers in the world are frequent and severe weather anomalies. Recent years have shown that climate changes, causing long lasting and devastating drought as well as recurring rainstorms are a huge risk factor for every farmer.

INTERMAG has created a series of AMINO ULTRA® fertilizers with GCAA technology, which is a response to the new challenges posed to agriculture by the progressive climate change.

SINGLE-COMPONENT FERTILIZERS

AMINO ULTRA® Ca-18, Cu-24, Fe-20, Mn-22, Zn-24 are single-component micronutrient fertilizers in a form of ultra- granule, intended for supplementing deficiencies of micronutrients in all types of crops. They effectively prevent and eliminate the symptoms of deficiencies of particular nutrients, ensuring proper plant growth and development.

MULTI-COMPONENT FERTILIZERS

AMINO ULTRA® CEREALS, OIL CROPS, CORN, HORTI are multi-component micronutrient fertilizers in a form of one ultra- granule, which compositions have been adjusted to the needs of particular groups of crops. Fertilizers quickly and effectively provide plants with micronutrients in proportions that meet their requirements.
